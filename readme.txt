# Custom Tax Plugin

**Description:** Add a dynamic 10% tax to the total in WooCommerce.

**Version:** 1.0

## Installation

1. Upload the `custom-tax-plugin` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

## Usage

### Custom Tax Settings

In the WooCommerce settings, you'll find a new option for configuring the custom tax percentage.

1. Navigate to **WooCommerce > Settings**.
2. Click on the **Tax** tab.
3. Find the section for **Standard Tax** (or your preferred tax class).
4. Locate the **Custom Tax Percentage** field and enter the desired percentage.
5. Save changes.

### Custom Tax Calculation

The plugin dynamically calculates a 10% tax based on the configured percentage and adds it to the cart total.
